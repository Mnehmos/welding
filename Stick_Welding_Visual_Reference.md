## COMMON ELECTRODE SELECTION GUIDE

| Electrode | Characteristics | Current Type | Best Applications |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| E6010     | ■ Deep penetration<br>■ Fast-freeze slag<br>■ Forceful arc | DC+ | • Root passes<br>• Pipe welding<br>• Dirty/rusty metal<br>• Vertical up |
| E6011     | ■ Similar to E6010<br>■ Good for AC power<br>■ Good penetration | AC or DC+ | • When only AC is available<br>• Field work<br>• Repair work<br>• Vertical up |
| E6013     | ■ Soft arc<br>■ Light penetration<br>■ Easy to control | AC or DC± | • Sheet metal<br>• Thin materials<br>• Auto body work<br>• Beginners |
| E7014     | ■ Iron powder coating<br>■ High deposition rate<br>■ Medium penetration | AC or DC± | • Filling large gaps<br>• High production work<br>• Flat/horizontal welds |
| E7018     | ■ Low hydrogen<br>■ Iron powder coating<br>■ Smooth, quiet arc | AC or DC+ | • Structural welding<br>• High-strength steel<br>• Critical welds<br>• Code work |
| E7024     | ■ Heavy iron powder<br>■ Very high deposition<br>■ "Drag rod" | AC or DC± | • Flat and horizontal only<br>• Production welding<br>• Filling large gaps<br>• Speed welding |

## TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK GUIDE

| Problem | Appearance | Causes | Solutions |
|---------|------------|--------|-----------|
| Porosity | Holes in weld bead | • Moisture<br>• Contamination<br>• Arc too long | • Clean material<br>• Proper electrode storage<br>• Correct arc length |
| Undercut | Groove in base metal | • Too high amperage<br>• Improper angle<br>• Too fast travel | • Reduce current<br>• Adjust angle<br>• Slow down |
| Overlap | Weld metal flows on base metal without fusion | • Insufficient heat<br>• Improper angle<br>• Too slow travel | • Increase amperage<br>• Adjust technique<br>• Proper travel speed |
| Lack of Fusion | Weld metal not fused to base metal | • Insufficient heat<br>• Poor technique<br>• Contamination | • Increase amperage<br>• Proper angles<br>• Clean material |
| Excessive Spatter | Metal droplets around weld | • Arc too long<br>• Amperage too high<br>• Wrong polarity | • Shorten arc length<br>• Adjust amperage<br>• Check polarity |
| Electrode Sticking | Electrode freezes to work | • Current too low<br>• Arc too short<br>• Poor technique | • Increase amperage<br>• Proper arc length<br>• Correct starting method |
